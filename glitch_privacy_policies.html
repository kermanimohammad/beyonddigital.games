Privacy Policy
GLITCH Game

Effective Date: Aug 01, 2025

1. Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This mobile game ("GLITCH") is developed to offer an enjoyable experience without collecting or storing personal information from users.
This Privacy Policy outlines what data we handle, what third-party services we use, and how purchases are managed.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect the following:
Personal identifiers (e.g., name, email, phone number)
Location data (GPS or network-based)
Contacts or social data
Photos, media, or files
Login credentials or account access
Device sensor data
You can play the game anonymously without signing in or submitting any personal information.

3. Third-Party Services (AdMob)

The game displays third-party advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads support our development and help keep the game free.
AdMob may collect non-personally identifiable information such as:
Device type and model
Operating system
Language settings
Anonymous Advertising ID

AdMob may use this data to serve personalized ads (based on your consent, where applicable). You can control ad personalization via your device settings.
Learn more: Google AdMob Privacy & Terms

4. In-App Purchases

GLITCH may offer optional in-app purchases. These purchases are handled securely via the Google Play billing system. We do not collect or store any payment information.

5. Children’s Privacy

This game is suitable for all age groups, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
Ads shown are appropriate for general audiences, and we comply with COPPA and GDPR-K.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no risk of data misuse from our side. However, third-party services like AdMob manage their own data and policies.

7. Your Consent

By playing GLITCH, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any part of it, please do not use the game.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may change in the future. If it does, users will be informed through an in-game notice or update description on Google Play.
