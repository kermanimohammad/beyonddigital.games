Privacy Policy
Sleep Game

Effective Date: May 26, 2025

1. Introduction
We are committed to protecting your privacy. This mobile game ("the Game") is developed to provide fun and engaging entertainment without collecting or storing any personal information from users.
This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data and what kind of third-party services we use, if any. 

2. Information We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect any of the following:
Personal identifiers (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
Location data (GPS or network-based)
Contacts or social data Photos, media, or files
Login credentials or account access Device sensor data
You can play the game completely
anonymously without signing in or providing any information.

3. Third-Party Services (AdMob)
The game includes third-party advertising via Google AdMob. These ads are intended to
support our development and keep the game free to play.
Google AdMob may collect non-personally identifiable information such as:
• Device type and model
• Operating system
• Language settings
• Anonymous Advertising ID

AdMob may use this information to show personalized ads (depending on your consent, where
applicable). You can control ad personalization through your device settings.
To learn more, please see:
Google AdMob Privacy & Terms

4. Children’s Privacy
This game is suitable for players of all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly
collect any personal information from children. Our use of AdMob is limited to content-appropriate ads, and we comply with relevant laws like COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy
Protection Act) and GDPR-K (General Data Protection Regulation for Kids).

5. Data Security
Since we do not collect or store user data, there is no risk of data breach or misuse on our end.
However, third-party services like AdMob have their own data handling policies, and we
encourage users to review those.

6. Your Consent
By using this game, you consent to this privacy policy. If you do not agree with any part of this
policy, please refrain from using the game.
