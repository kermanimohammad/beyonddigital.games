Privacy Policy
Word Sphere

Effective Date:  July 1, 2026

Welcome to Word Sphere.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Word Sphere handles information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

Word Sphere does not collect any personally identifiable information such as:

Name
Email address
Phone number
Home address
Contacts
Photos
Messages

The game can be played without creating an account.

Game Progress

Your game progress, unlocked levels, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

We do not upload this information to any server.

Advertising

If advertisements are included in the game, they may be provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect anonymous information such as:

Device type
Advertising ID
Language
General location (country/region)
Crash reports
App performance information

Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy:

https://beyonddigital.games/wordsphere_privacypolicies.html

Purchases

If the game offers in-app purchases, all payments are securely processed by Google Play.

We do not receive or store your payment information.

Analytics

Word Sphere may use anonymous analytics tools to improve gameplay and fix bugs.

These tools do not identify you personally.

Children's Privacy

Word Sphere is designed for players of all ages.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe personal information has been collected unintentionally, please contact us so it can be removed.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information used by the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact
