Privacy Policy
Reaver Protocol

Effective Date: Oct 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users. However, we use third-party services such as AdMob and Google Play Games that may collect certain non-personal information, including:

Device Information: Information such as your device’s model, operating system version, and device identifiers.
Advertising Information: Data related to your interaction with advertisements displayed in the game, which helps us provide personalized ads.

2. Use of Personal Data

While our game does not directly collect personal data, the third-party services integrated into the game may collect and use certain data, such as:

AdMob: Google’s advertising platform collects information to serve personalized ads. For more details, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy here.
Google Play Games: If you choose to log in or connect with Google Play Games, it may collect data related to your gaming activity.

3. Data Access Permissions

Our game may request the following permissions from your device:

Storage Access: The game requires access to your device’s storage to save game data, such as game progress, settings, and preferences.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

AdMob: We use AdMob by Google to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your data for ad targeting purposes. You can find more information about how AdMob uses your data in its Privacy Policy.
Google Play Games: By integrating Google Play Games, we may access data for purposes such as leaderboards and achievements, but this service is entirely optional.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our game is intended for adults only, and we do not knowingly collect or solicit any personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child, we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Retention

We retain data collected by third-party services only for as long as it is necessary to provide our services. Data retention policies may vary depending on the third-party services we use, so please review the respective privacy policies of AdMob and Google Play Games.

7. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us through the game. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated “Effective Date.” We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Internet Connection Requirement

Please note that our game requires an active internet connection to function properly. You must have internet access enabled on your device in order to play the game and use certain features, such as achievements, and advertisements.
