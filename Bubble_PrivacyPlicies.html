Privacy Policy
Bubbles Game

Effective Date: January 09, 2025

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This document explains how our game handles user data.
1. Data Collection
Our game does not collect any personal information or data from users. We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

2. Advertisements
Our game uses AdMob to display advertisements. Please note that ad providers may use general device information, such as the advertising ID, to show relevant ads. For more information about AdMob’s privacy policies, visit Google AdMob Privacy Policies.

3. In-App Purchases
Our game includes in-app purchases managed through the secure Google Play payment gateway. All payment-related information is handled directly by Google Play, and we do not have access to any payment details.

4. Children Under 13
Our game is designed for children under 13 years of age. We do not collect any user data, ensuring a safe and appropriate experience for younger players.

5. Data Security
Since no user information is collected, no additional security measures are required.

6. Changes to This Policy
This policy is fixed and will not change in the future.

7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us via email: [najiforex8118@gmail.com]

8. Firebase Usage
This game uses Firebase services provided by Google. Firebase is implemented solely for the purpose of analyzing user behavior and improving gameplay performance. The data collected may include nonpersonally identifiable information such as device type, gameplay duration, and in-game events. No personally identifiable information is collected or shared. The information gathered is used only in aggregate to understand user behavior and enhance the overall user experience.
